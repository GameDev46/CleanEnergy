# Outer Wilds - Clean Energy



<hr>

## Info

With Climate Change becoming an ever present issue, the Hearthians decided it was time to go green. Their first step was to replace your ship's fuel with much more environmentally friendly solar power!

However, the ship's battery is only so large meaning travellers will need to watch their fuel much more carefully, especially when their ship is out of view or far away from the sun.

## Usage

The mod replaces the ship's fuel with solar power, which is generated based on the ship's distance to the sun and whether the sun is in view.

The solar panel's efficiency can be changed in settings to be either Low, Medium, High or Ultra.
